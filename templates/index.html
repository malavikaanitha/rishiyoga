Rishi Yoga and Meditation Academy, renowned as a leading institution for traditional yoga education in India, holds extensive experience in imparting knowledge. With a focus on the systematic and classical style of Indian Hatha yoga, we are dedicated to spreading the authentic essence of this ancient science.

Our primary objective is to promote the practice and understanding of yoga in its purest form. To date, our academy has successfully trained more than 500 individuals from around the world in the disciplines of yoga and meditation.
