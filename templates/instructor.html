YOGACHARYA RISHIKESH SAKALESH

He is an international yoga coach certified by yoga alliance and various other International,National and Government organisations. He is born into a traditional yoga family and has shown immense interest in yoga and spirituality since childhood. He has undergone yoga and meditation trainings from various experts and gurus in the field of traditional yoga. He is also a traditional yogic energy healer . His teaching style is simple, easy and flexible. He has gone deep into the core of Indian traditional yoga and his mission is to make others experience the real authentic Indian yoga. He teaches yoga in a holistic manner that suits the individual needs and has been conducting sessions and workshops for organizations, corporate, monasteries and many nonprofit centers.

YOGACHARYA LATHIKA SAKALESH

She is born and raised in Kerala, Gods own country. It is her vision and sincere effort that resulted in the establishment of the yoga academy. Her whole life is dedicated towards sharing of true yogic experience. She has studied yoga from various schools in India. She is highly experienced in teaching core hatha yoga, pranayama and many ancient meditation techniques and is a spiritual counsellor as well. She has received various awards and honors from many organisations and featured in many magazines, television channels and various yoga seminars throughout India.

YOGACHARYA TANAYA ROY CHOUDHURI

She is a beacon of serenity and wisdom. She will lead you on a transformative journey towards self-discovery and inner peace. With a decade of experience in London's Banking and Media industry, Tanaya's deep connection to yoga has shaped her path. She specializes in various Yogasana forms, including Hatha, Vinyasa, Qigong, Therapeutic Yogasana, and Pre and Postnatal recovery, offering a well-rounded approach to cater to diverse needs. Under her guidance, expect a holistic yoga experience covering physical postures, alignment, breathwork, meditation, and diet. She fosters an inclusive environment for students of all levels to thrive, cultivating mindfulness, flexibility, and well-being. Tanaya's experience and compassionate teaching style will elevate your physical and spiritual well-being.

Aastha Sharma's primary focus is on yoga therapy, which aims to enhance physical and mental well-being.
